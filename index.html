D.I.Y (Does It Interest You) is an independent research and publishing project that explores the book as a container for ideas. D.I.Y focuses on nothing in particular other than what the author finds interesting at the time of an issue’s making. Each issue is published as a downloadable PDF file that can be printed and assembled at home. For collaborations and other inquiries, please get in touch here.
