D.I.Y

D.I.Y (Does It Interest You) is an independent research and publishing project by Qiang John Wang. D.I.Y focuses on nothing in particular other than what the author finds interesting at the time of an issue’s making. Each issue is published as a downloadable PDF file that can be printed and assembled at home.
